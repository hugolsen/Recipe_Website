The Best Pancakes Ever

Ingredients

  • 10 ounces flour
  • 3 Tbsp sugar
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 vegetable oil, and extra for the pan
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • Butter and syrup for serving

About

This is a recipe from the YouTuber Binging with Babish. I first made this recipe for my mom and sister when they had COVID in 2020. They became my favorite pancakes and I have used the recipe every time since when I make pancakes.

Prep

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cooking Time: 10 minutes

Steps

  1. In one bowl, combine all of the dry ingredients. Whisk to combine.
  2. In a seperate bowl, combine the wet ingredients. This is also where the optional vanilla extract would be added. Whisk to combine.
  3. Combine both bowls of ingredients and gently mix it with a rubber spatula. There should still be some dry streaks/lumps, and it is very important to not overmix your pancakes.
  4. Cover the batter and let it rest at room temp for 10 minutes.
  5. Heat 1 teaspoon of vegetable oil in a nonstick skillet.
  6. Ladle one scoop of batter on to the skillet and flip it once bubbles appear around the edge of the pancake. Always cook one pancake first because the first pancake is always the worst.
  7. After cooking all of the batter, stack your pancakes up and enjoy with your desired toppings.
